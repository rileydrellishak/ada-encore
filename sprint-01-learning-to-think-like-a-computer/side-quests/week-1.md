# 🌱 Week 1 Side Quests: Playing With Clarity

## 🌱 What are side quests?

Side quests are optional explorations.

They are not required.

They are not graded.

They exist because curiosity is part of engineering.

---

## 🎯 Side Quest 1 — Rewrite for clarity

Take any function you wrote this week and:

* rename variables more clearly
* simplify logic
* remove unnecessary steps

Then compare versions.

Ask:

> Which one feels easier to read?

---

## 🧠 Side Quest 2 — Predict before running

Before executing any code:

* write down what you think will happen
* then run it
* compare expectation vs reality

Focus:

> training prediction accuracy

---

## 🧪 Side Quest 3 — Break it intentionally

Take working code and:

* introduce a small bug
* observe the error
* debug it using your workflow

Goal:

> build familiarity with failure

---

## 🔁 Side Quest 4 — Solve it again later

Pick any assignment solution and:

* redo it from scratch after 24–48 hours
* compare your approaches

Ask:

* did your solution get simpler?
* did you think differently?

---

## 🌿 Side Quest 5 — “Explain it to Future You”

Write a short explanation of one function as if:

> Future you forgot everything about this code

Keep it simple.

No jargon.

---

## 🧭 Philosophy reminder

Side quests are where you:

* build intuition
* experiment safely
* reinforce learning without pressure

They are not extra work.

They are optional depth.
